What is Bread?

According to Merriam-Webster, bread is "a usually baked and leavened food made of a mixture whose basic constituent is flour or meal". Due to this, bread has many healthy benefits!

Some of these benefits are but not limited to...

  • Contains Fiber
  • Contains Protein
  • Fuels Your Body
  • Low In Fat
  • Can Lower the Risk Of Cancer

But Which Bread Shall I Consume?

There are many different types of bread, and some types of break are much healthier than others. For example, whole wheat bread contains more fiber, has a higher nutritional value, and may have lower calories.
